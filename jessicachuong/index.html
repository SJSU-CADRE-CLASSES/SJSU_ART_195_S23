Feelin' Snackish

Feelin' Snackish is a 3d virtual installation based on two of my favorite Asian snacks from childhood: White Rabbit Candy and Dried Squid. This 3d world evokes cuteness and wonder to recreate a childlike imaginative experience.

A main theme that recurs in my art is the theme of cuteness. I always been drawn to cute things, and now I want to create them.
