GRID OF LIFE

Grid of Life represents my exploration and anticipation of the future. Through this interactive art installation, I envision myself playing a role in a cloud-based virtual world in the next ten years. Within this 8 by 10 matrix, I will become a digitized entity, occupying less than 100 bytes of space. This work embodies my imagination of the future and my pursuit of the limitless possibilities of the intersection between technology and art. I believe that as technology continues to evolve, the boundary between our reality and the virtual world will become increasingly blurred, and I will become a part of this new era. Through this work, I hope to inspire viewers to think about the future, triggering their reflection and exploration of the digital age.
