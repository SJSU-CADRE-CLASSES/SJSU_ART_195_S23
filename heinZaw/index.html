Cycle of Being

Have you ever looked into the depths of your soul and asked yourself: what is the meaning of life? Have you ever felt the weight of monotony, as if you were trapped in an endless cycle? Or have you ever thought about whether life has lost all meaning?



"The end of life is death, it is emptiness, and there is no meaning at the end. So we have to say,
there is meaning in the process. But when the process betrays us, we look at the end again and comfort ourselves and say,
