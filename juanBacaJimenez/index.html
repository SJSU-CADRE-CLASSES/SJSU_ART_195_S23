Natural and Digital Bloom

Appreciating the beauty of artificial and natural plants.

Natural and Digital Bloom

Natural and Digital Bloom includes both real organic elements and digitally produced images. It plays with the idea of expressing beauty from the natural world, but also how beautiful the result of an artificial element can be displayed after being created through virtual space. Also, the natural aspect for this work is to bring the essence of being around nature for the audience, but also being surrounded by the digital realm of things because that's the world we live in. It is very challenging, yet beautiful at the same time to live between both real and virtual worlds.
